How to order

  • Order via Hotline: Call directly to the phone number: 037.905.8911 for quick assistance from our staff regarding products and services.
  • Order on FB: Send a picture or the product code you are interested in to the KRIK Page (m.me/krik.vn). Our staff will respond as quickly as possible. For immediate assistance, contact the hotline at 0379.058.911.
  • Order on the Website:
    • Step 1: Click on the product you want to order.
    • Step 2: Choose the color, size, and quantity you want, then click [Buy Now].
    • Step 3: Click on [Proceed to Checkout] to go to the shipping information form or you can click on [View Cart] to review your cart.
    • Step 4: Fill in the information in the order form: Full name, phone number, email, address.
    • Step 5: Choose the payment method: COD (payment upon delivery).
    • Step 6: Complete the order.
  • KRIK will confirm the order by calling. Please answer the call so that we can successfully process your order.
